How to Play

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic game where two players take turns marking the spaces in a 3x3 grid. The first player to get three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.

  • Objective: Get three of your marks (X or O) in a row.
  • Turns: Players alternate placing their marks on the grid.
  • Winning: The first player to align three marks in a row wins.
